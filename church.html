ABOUT US

A Christ-Centered Church Built On Faith, Worship & Love

Cherish Chapel Worldwide is a welcoming Christian ministry dedicated to transforming lives through worship, prayer, biblical teaching, and genuine fellowship.

We believe in creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God, grow spiritually, and discover their divine purpose in Christ.

Our Mission

To lead people into a deeper relationship with God through worship, prayer, and biblical teaching.

Our Vision

To impact lives globally with hope, salvation, and transformation through Jesus Christ.

Our Values

Faith, love, unity, excellence, holiness, and service remain our foundation.
